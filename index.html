Rome

The Eternal City

Rome is so multilayered with history that you will discover ancient architecture,artistic virtuosity and fantastical fountains around every corner.Its also surprisingly walkable with only the occational bus,metro or taxi necessary.With so many neighbourhoods,landmarks and famous works of art,its impossible to enjoy everything the capital has to offer in a single visit.But with the right guidance,you can certainly soak up as many sights,sounds ans Spritz as time allows.Whether you are here for the art,the architecture or the wonderful cuisine,we hope you enjoy your visit.

~benvenuti a roma!
